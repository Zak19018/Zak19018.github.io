Description

The target audience of Welly Weather includes:

  • Who: Parents, students, athletes, anyone who goes outside.
  • Age: Adolescent - Middle Aged (roughly 15 - 50 years old)
  • Tech: Familiar with technology and how to use it to their advantage. Use their mobile devices as planning and productivity tools, as well as for entertainment.
  • Income: Wages vary greatly by industry. On average, $50 000 annually.
  • Motivation: Seeking resources for planning trips, activities, or what to wear. Also looking for a bit of entertainment while doing so.
