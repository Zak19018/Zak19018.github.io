Site Purpose

The main purpose of Welly Weather will be to provide information to users about the weather - basic temperatures and forecasts, as well as other information on storms, swimming and/or surfing, and fishing conditions. What makes Welly Weather different from other weather providers is that it will present this information in a fresh, exciting and entertaining way. Not just the essentials, but other things to keep you going and brighten up your day.
