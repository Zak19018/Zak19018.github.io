Typography

The selling-point of Welly Weather is to take something that is ordinary, like a weather report, and make it fun and entertaining. There is always a level of professionalism involved and not everything will be light-hearted. I wanted to combine a serif and sans-serif font pairing for contrast. The serif to bring in that professionalism - like a newspaper headline. And the sans serif for readability and to show the true character of the site. These are the selected fonts:

Element Font Size
Header Jost 1.5rem (24px)
Navigation Libre Caslon Text 0.9rem (14.4px)
Footer Jost 0.9rem (14.4px)
Headings Libre Caslon Text 1.5rem (24px)
Paragraphs Jost 16px
