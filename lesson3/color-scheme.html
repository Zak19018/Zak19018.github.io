Color Scheme

Welly Weather is named after the capital city of New Zealand - Wellington. Wellington's official colors are black and gold. I wanted to play on those colors while maintaining a light and fun design. I chose gold to be the primary color with mostly black accents. And of course that can get a bit tiring to look at, so as an additional accent color I used a royal blue.
