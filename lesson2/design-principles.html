White Space & Clean Design

Apple

apple.com/za

Apple are known for great designs. We see that implemented here on their website. The focal point being the iPhone 11 Pro and it's camera. Although the space around the image and text is not "white", the black background is negative space that allows the user's eyes to rest, as well as focus on the most important information on the page - their latest product of course.

PARC: Repetition

Trello

> trello.com

Trello is a great productivity tool. In order to be productive, you need simplicity and familiarity - or at least in this case. Trello's design repeats the rectangle shape with rounded edges, much like what we copied for our Assignment Portal home page. This repetition creates a sense of unity on their website and with their brand. As you make use of their tools, you find yourself becoming accustomed to these rectangular "cards".
